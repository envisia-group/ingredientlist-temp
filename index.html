Why We Exist

Supplement Labels Shouldn't Be a Mystery

Every year, millions of people buy dietary supplements without fully understanding what's inside them. Labels are small, ingredient names are unfamiliar, and finding reliable, unbiased information shouldn't require a chemistry degree.

No sponsored rankings. No marketing spin. Just clear, factual ingredient data sourced directly from each manufacturer.

IngredientList was built to solve that problem. We're creating the most comprehensive, searchable supplement ingredient database on the web — a single place where anyone can look up, compare, and research exactly what's in the products they take every day.
