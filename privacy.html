1. Overview

IngredientList ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website ingredientlist.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site.

IngredientList is a free, informational supplement ingredient database. We are not an e-commerce platform and do not sell supplements or health products. Our address is 300 SE 2nd St, Suite 601, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33301.

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address — submitted through our waitlist or early access signup form

We do not collect your name, payment information, health data, or any other personal details through the signup form.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Cookie identifiers and similar tracking identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Email address: To send waitlist confirmations, early access notifications, and occasional product updates related to IngredientList. Every email includes an unsubscribe option.
  • Analytics data: To understand how visitors use the Site, improve content and navigation, diagnose technical issues, and measure site performance.
  • IP address and device data: For security monitoring, fraud prevention, and aggregate demographic analysis.

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, sell it to third parties, or create individual user profiles for commercial purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the Site to function properly (e.g., session management).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Site (e.g., Google Analytics). These cookies collect information in an aggregated, anonymous form.

We do not use advertising, retargeting, or marketing cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on the Site.

If we use Google Analytics, you can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with a limited number of third-party service providers who assist in operating the Site. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information:

  • Analytics provider (e.g., Google Analytics) — receives anonymized usage data to help us understand site traffic and behavior.
  • Email service provider — processes email addresses collected through the waitlist to deliver communications on our behalf.
  • Hosting provider — server logs may include IP addresses and are retained for a limited period for security and operational purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, in response to a valid legal process (such as a subpoena or court order), or to protect the rights, property, or safety of IngredientList, our users, or the public.

In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets), user data may be transferred. We will notify users if this occurs.

6. Data Retention

  • Email addresses: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until the waitlist is closed.
  • Analytics data: Retained per the default settings of our analytics provider (typically 14 months for user-level data).
  • Server logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and operational purposes.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit, access controls for internal systems, and the use of reputable third-party infrastructure providers.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request to know what personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us directly. We will honor requests within 10 business days.
  • Cookie opt-out: Manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within 45 days of receiving your request.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA):

  • Right to know the categories of personal information we collect and the purposes for which it is used.
  • Right to deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

Under California's "Shine the Light" law (Civil Code Section 1798.83), we confirm that we do not share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has submitted personal information (such as an email address), we will delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
