Our Story

Why We Built IngredientList

It started with a simple question: "What's actually in this?"

We were standing in a supplement aisle, staring at dozens of bottles with tiny labels and unfamiliar ingredients. We wanted to compare products, understand what we were buying, and find something that matched our specific needs. But there was nowhere to go.

Every search led to sponsored "top 10" lists, affiliate review sites, or forums full of conflicting opinions. No one was just showing us the ingredients. No one was making it easy to compare. No one was giving us the facts without an agenda.

We decided to build the resource we wished existed. Not a review site. Not a marketplace. Just the facts.

IngredientList is a database built from the ground up to serve one purpose: give people direct access to what's inside the supplements they take. Every ingredient list sourced directly from each manufacturer. Every product categorized with precision. Every search designed for the way real people actually think.

We don't sell supplements. We don't accept sponsored placements. We don't make health claims. We just organize the data and make it searchable — so you can make informed decisions on your own terms.
